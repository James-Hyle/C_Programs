A foot rheostat is available for variable speed  control.  
To connect,  join moTor cord plug with rheostat
plug.  see parts  list.

ASSEMBLY INSTRUCTIONS  --  The  base  and Column are
assembled together at the Factory, as are  also the 
motor and table bracket assemblies.  Slide the table
bracket  assembly first on the column,  position and
Tighten.   The motor assembly is then slid on the 
Column. Line up the Motor with  the Table and Tighten
temporarily with hand wheel. Insert drill into chuck 
 and line  up drill with hole in center of Table.
